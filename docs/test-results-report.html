⚠️ Problem Statement

Current Pain Points

  • 706 servers monitored across 158+ client accounts (AWS + GCP)
  • All alerts routed to a single email inbox — no prioritization
  • Every alert requires manual triage, troubleshooting, and server restart
  • On-call fatigue across a 20–25 person operations team
  • No correlation between alerts, logs, and past incidents
  • Repetitive client communication after every incident

Target Outcomes

  • AI classifies and triages every alert automatically
  • Known patterns auto-remediated without human touch
  • Tickets auto-created, updated, and closed per incident
  • Incident summaries auto-sent to team + client
  • Humans paged only for unknown/high-risk situations
  • Full audit trail of every action taken

🔧 Auto-Remediate

Service restart → if fails → EC2 restart (tag-gated: auto-restart=true). Zero human intervention for known patterns.

🎫 Create Ticket

ManageEngine ticket auto-created with AI-generated summary. Assigned to on-call. No triage needed.

🚨 Escalate

Immediate human notification + high-priority ticket. Reserved for unknown/critical/destructive events.
